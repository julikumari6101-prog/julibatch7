Our Story

Founded in 2010, The Golden Fork has been serving exceptional cuisine crafted from locally-sourced ingredients. Our award-winning chef brings over 20 years of experience from kitchens around the world.


Every dish tells a story of passion, creativity, and dedication to culinary excellence. We believe dining should be an experience that engages all senses.
